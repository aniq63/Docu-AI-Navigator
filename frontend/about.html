How It Works

DocuAI Navigator combines document management, databases, and AI-driven reasoning into one seamless system:

  1. Register & Login: Companies register, log in, and set up their digital workspace.
  2. Upload Documents: PDF and project files are securely uploaded, validated, and processed.
  3. AI Processing: Documents are chunked, embedded, and stored in a vector database for retrieval.
  4. Project Creation: Define projects, teams, and goals with customizable forms.
  5. Project Planning: AI generates detailed plans based on domain, team size, roles, and tech stack.
  6. Ask & Retrieve: Teams query documents in natural language, and DocuAI provides intelligent responses.

AI & Technology

DocuAI Navigator uses a multi-LLM architecture:

  • LLM-1 (Extraction): Identifies names, metadata, and document context.
  • LLM-2 (RAG): Handles document Q&A with retrieval-augmented generation.
  • LLM-3 (Planning): Generates structured, actionable project roadmaps.

Backed by vector databases, document chunking, and embeddings, our system ensures fast, accurate, and scalable AI-driven insights.
